About this Portal

Built for Adjunct Faculty in ICP

The Integral Counseling Psychology (ICP) program at CIIS trains therapists rooted in depth psychology, somatic awareness, and social justice. Adjunct professors are the backbone of this community, and this portal is designed to make your administrative and pedagogical life as smooth as possible.

Content on each page serves as a living placeholder — program staff and faculty coordinators will continue to expand and update each section.
