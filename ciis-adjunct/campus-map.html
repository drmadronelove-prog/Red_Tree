1

Ground Floor

Entry Level

Welcome to CIIS

The ground floor is where community life begins — arrive, decompress, grab a coffee, and connect with colleagues before class.

  • 🛋️
    Student & Faculty Lounge A welcoming common area for faculty and students to relax, meet, and debrief between classes. Comfortable seating, natural light, and community bulletin boards with program news and events.
  • ☕
    CIIS Café Coffee, tea, pastries, and light snacks available on weekdays. Hours vary by semester — check posted signage. A great spot for informal student-faculty conversations.
  • 🚪
    Main Lobby & Reception Building reception, visitor check-in, and campus information desk. Mail and packages are received here.
  • 📌
    Community Bulletin Board Announcements, events, student resources, and ICP program updates are posted here throughout the semester.
2

Second Floor

Library & Learning

Knowledge & Research

The heart of CIIS academic life, the library houses specialized collections in psychology, philosophy, religion, somatic studies, and social justice.

  • 📚
    CIIS Library Full library with physical collections, reference desk staffed by librarians, and quiet study areas. Specializes in transpersonal psychology, depth psychology, world philosophies, and somatic studies. Open to all faculty and enrolled students.
  • 🖥️
    Computer Workstations Library computers with access to all CIIS digital databases, printing, and scanning services.
  • 🤫
    Quiet Study Rooms Individual and small-group study rooms available for student use. Check availability at the reference desk.
  • 📖
    Course Reserves Required course readings placed on reserve by faculty. Submit reserve requests at least 3 weeks in advance through the library.

3

Third Floor

Gathering & Nourishment

Community & Practice Space

The third floor hosts the largest gathering and event spaces in the building, as well as student dining.

  • 🙏
    Namaste Hall CIIS's primary large event and gathering space. Used for all-school assemblies, guest lectures, somatic workshops, movement practices, and ceremonial gatherings. Flexible floor plan with removable furniture. Shoes typically removed at the entrance.
  • 🍱
    Student Cafeteria The main student dining area with tables, chairs, and access to microwaves and a refrigerator. Students often gather here between classes. Vending machines available. Cafeteria hours follow the academic calendar.
  • 🧘
    Meditation & Practice Room A designated quiet space for mindfulness, meditation, and brief somatic practice. Available to faculty and students throughout the day.
Namaste Hall booking process and cafeteria hours to be added here.
4

Fourth Floor

Faculty Space

Faculty Resources

The fourth floor is primarily faculty space — a place to decompress, prep, and connect with colleagues away from the classroom.

  • 🛋️
    Faculty Lounge A dedicated rest and preparation space for faculty. Features comfortable seating, a small kitchen area with coffee and tea, workstations, and a printer. A great place to arrive early, review your notes, or debrief with colleagues after a session.
  • 🚻
    Restrooms Accessible, all-gender restrooms are located on this floor. Additional restrooms are available on other floors — check floor signage for locations.
  • 📋
    Faculty Mailboxes Adjunct faculty may be assigned a mailbox here. Check with the Program Coordinator about mailbox access for adjunct instructors.
  • 💬
    Faculty Meeting Room Small conference room available for faculty consultation, student meetings, and peer consultation. Contact the Program Coordinator to reserve.
5

Fifth Floor

Administration

Administrative Hub

The fifth floor is the administrative center of CIIS — where payroll gets processed, contracts are signed, and institutional operations are coordinated.

  • 👥
    Human Resources The HR office handles all adjunct faculty contracts, payroll setup, direct deposit, benefits enrollment, and personnel matters. Open Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm. Come here to complete your onboarding paperwork.
  • ⚙️
    Operations & Facilities CIIS building operations, room reservations, facilities requests, and maintenance issues are managed here. If you have a facilities concern in your classroom, contact Operations.
  • 📊
    Finance & Business Office Handles institutional finances, reimbursements, and budget matters. Contact here for expense reimbursement requests.
  • 🏛️
    Academic Affairs / Provost's Office Oversees academic policy, faculty governance, and curriculum. Escalate unresolved academic concerns to this office through your Program Coordinator.
